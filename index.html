Why?
Businesses want more customers, customers want better products, and marketers want better ways of channeling communication between the customers and businesses. What all marketers seek most from consumers is their honest opinion about good and services. Today, these honest opinions are most prominent within the social network. These opinions are mostly generated voluntarily, and therefore are considered unadulterated. However, marketers are struggling to utilize these data to their advantage.
Difficulties in Social Data Analysis
Vast and Volatile Information
Noise Saturation

Problems with Existing Social Big Data Analysis Service
Over 70% of the opinions on the social network are concentrated around the reference point
Does not consider the distinctiveness of the opinions
Unable to properly analyze opinions
Unable to provide accurate information about consumer opinions
in-DataLab's Mission
Provide marketers with Social Big Data that are collected, analyzed, visualized based on automation algorithm.
Help companies better understand consumer needs and help the consumers get the products they want by facilitating communication between companies and consumers.
